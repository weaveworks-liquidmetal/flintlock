Flintlock

A streamlined service to manage the lifecycle of microVMs. Flintlock lets you focus on deploying your application in MicroVMs tailored for its need.

Backed by Container Images

With flintlock, you can use OCI images to supply kernel binaries and Operating Systems to your VMs; no more large and cumbersome filesystem images.
